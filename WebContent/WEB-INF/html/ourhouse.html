Our House
    In 2005 we were given the opportunity to be the first sorority on the Greek Quad. We are located at 1077 Morewood Avenue, a walk across the street to campus.

    We recently redesigned and renovated the entire first floor of our house courtesy of the renowned interior designer, Karen Ledbetter, a Theta alumna. The house features 6 single rooms and 11 double rooms, allowing housing for 28 sisters. The first floor is equipped with a spacious kitchen, complete with all necessary cooking and baking supplies. The casual living room has a large flat-screen TV. A second formal living room provides space for meetings and schoolwork. Additionally, there is a study room on the first floor for those who prefer a quiet place to work. In the basement there is an exercise and chapter room.
