Scholarship

Academic Strength
We believe that academics should be a priority for every member of the house. We provide an in house tutoring system, study hours, academic mentors, and a study room. We require all our members to have a minimum 3.0 high school GPA and a 2.5 college GPA. Our GPA is consistently above the University average. Kappa Alpha Theta has had the highest GPA among Panhellenic Greek organizations at CMU 9 out of the last 12 semesters. Theta’s average GPA has also been above the all-women’s GPA since all-women’s statistics were recorded in 2001.

Scholarship Banquet
To honor the hard work Thetas do the Scholarship Committee hosts a Scholarship banquet every semester where sisters are recognized for high achievement in their academic endeavors. Sisters invite a professor or faculty member who has facilitated a healthy learning environment and helped them on the road to success. Sisters who achieve a 4.0 semester GPA receive a scholarship pearl to attach to their Theta pin.
