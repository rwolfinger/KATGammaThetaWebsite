Here at Carnegie Mellon, Kappa Alpha Theta (or simply "Theta," for short) is comprised of remarkable women who strive for excellence in scholarship, leadership, and service and seek to make the widest impact for good, while always strengthening our bonds of love. Theta sisterhood goes beyond mere friendship; it is a lifelong bond shared by women who share the joy of compassion, love, and loyalty.

To navigate our website, use the links above. We hope you enjoy exploring and getting to know what Theta is all about!
