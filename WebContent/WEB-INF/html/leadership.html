Leadership and Involvement

There are over 50 organizations that Thetas are involved in and approximately 70% of Thetas are in a leadership position at CMU. This year in orientation alone, 7 of our sisters are Orientation Counselors, 2 are Orientation Leaders, 1 is a Head Orientation Counselor and 3 are Resident Assistants. Theta offers 24 officer positions, which allows for our sisters be involved with the sorority as well as develop into being a leader. By the time each sister graduates she will have obtained at least one leadership position in Theta.
