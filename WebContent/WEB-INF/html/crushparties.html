Twice a year, Theta hosts a Crush Party to which sisters can invite a date on whom they have a "crush" - or just a friend! - for a semi-formal night of good conversation, good friends, and good food. We rent out a location near campus and have the entire restaurant or venue to enjoy a fun night. Last fall we enjoyed an evening at Union Grill on Craig Street, and our spring Crush Party took place at the classy Alto Lounge in Shadyside!
