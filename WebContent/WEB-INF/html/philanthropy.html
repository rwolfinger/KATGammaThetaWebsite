Philanthropy is one of the cornerstones of Kappa Alpha Theta. We host and participate in numerous philanthropy events throughout the year, as well as completing hundreds of service hours per semester.

CASA
In 1989, Kappa Alpha Theta Fraternity adopted National CASA (Court Appointed Special Advocates) as its national philanthropy. CASA volunteers are trained community volunteers who speak on behalf of abused and neglected children in court. They research the child's background details to help the court make a sound decision about the child's future. Since 1989, the Kappa Alpha Theta Foundation has provided grant support and contributed more than $900,000 to CASA.

At CMU, Thetas are proud to give their time and efforts to raising money and awareness for CASA. Each year we hold a variety of events to support CASA, including Winter Toy Drives, "Cupcakes for CASA," and "CASA Coffeehouse." This past spring, Gamma Theta participated in the CASA 5k (pictured left), a superhero-themed marathon which raised money for CASA.

Mr. University
Since 1985, Theta's signature philanthropy event has been the Mr. University Pageant. Each fall, this pageant showcases one man from each fraternity or any other campus organization in a variety of areas of competition including formal wear, swimsuit, toga wear, and talent. Mr. University has become a staple annual event at Carnegie Mellon full of humor, house pride, and healthy competition. All proceeds are donated to the Breathing Association in honor of one of our sisters, Karin Johnson, who died of an acute asthma attack.

Service
Sisters are required to complete at least 5 hours of service per semester, but the majority contribute much more of their time to various service organizations in order to bring the widest influence for good to the Pittsburgh community. Halloween on the Quad, 1000+, Relay for Life, and Strong Women Strong Girls are just a few of the events and organizations to which CMU Thetas contribute.

Other Events
Be on the lookout for our other fun philanthropy events: Kicks for CASA, CASA Coffeehouse, and Zumba for CASA!
