Greek Sing is a philanthropy event in which sororities and fraternities create 13-minute renditions of existing or original musicals and compete to take home first place, either as a single chapter or a doubles pair. The performance combines the talents of performing arts majors as well as Greeks who have never set foot on stage. The event raises money for a charity chosen by the Greek community each year. In Spring 2012, CMU Greek Life raised over $55,000 for the Children's Institute of Pittsburgh.

In Spring 2010, Theta teamed up with neighbor fraternity Pi Kappa Alpha to bring home first again with Mamma Mia! Last year, Theta shone in an original musical Spice Up Your Life, a hilarious and lively story of the Spice Girls and Justin Bieber teaming up to perform at CMU Carnival, and took home first place, in the singles category, for a third time! This past spring, Theta and fraternity Sigma Phi Epsilon took home the second place trophy in the doubles category for Across The Universe.
