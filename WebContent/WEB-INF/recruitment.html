All women interested in joining a sorority participate in a structured recruitment process called FMR (Formal Membership Recruitment). During this process, potential new members get to meet the women from each organization while making friends with the girls in their recruitment groups. We encourage anyone even remotely interested in Greek Life to sign up for recruitment!
